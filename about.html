About Us

The focal point of Justice without borders is promoting peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development.

We are a professional transparent team, with over 2 years of increasingly responsible experience in giving legal advice, conflict management, mental health awareness as well as implementing justice. At JWB we ensure that your services are provided by an amazing team of qualified professionals, this include, psychologists, lawyers, pastors and social workers. Our friendly professionals thrive to ensure public access to information and protect fundamental freedoms.

OVERVIEW

JWB was founded in 2023 as a medium to catapult good security sector governance for the procurement of the 2030 agenda enshrined by the United Nations General Assembly.It is an organization that thrives to, promote the rule of law and ensure equal access to justice and peace through the development of effective, accountable and transparent institutions with the help of qualified professionals.
